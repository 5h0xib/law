What We Do

Legal & Board Advisory

Advising on a range of transactions and negotiations including: finance and investment, commercial arrangements, regulatory matters including data privacy, outsourcing, cloud governance, market entry and regulator approvals on new products or licenses with a focus in the GCC countries and North Africa.

Sustainability & ESG Strategies

Including Net Zero decarbonization action plan (MVR), sustainable finance frameworks / KPI setting, environmental, social and governance (ESG) compliance plans, audits and benchmarking.

Capacity Building

Training & facilitating on ESG, diversity and inclusion, cultural intelligence for stakeholder engagement, impact and credibility.

Cultural Engagements & Events

Cross-culture programs and events on topics including the Arab region business and social etiquette, managing diverse workforce, handling microaggressions; MC-ing, moderating and interviewing capabilities.

Who We Are

Layla El-Wafi

Lead Actionist | Finance Lawyer | Board Advisor

Founding partner of Actionism Advisory. A senior lawyer and board advisor in corporate and banking law, sustainability and impact. Her advice considers the legal and social license, ensuring that organizations protect their regulatory standing and reputation by combining IQ/EQ/CQ, bilingual skills (EN/AR) and cultural fluency with 20+ years in law, finance and human rights across sectors and regions.

Her voice is both professional and creative – equally at home in a corporate boardroom or on stage performing spoken word poetry. Layla is an English law qualified lawyer with 17 years combined experience in international private practice and in-house counsel advising on high value and cross-border transactions, commercial contract negotiation, regulatory compliance and sustainability across UK / Europe, Singapore, GCC countries and North Africa regions.

Within financial services Layla has experience related to new market and product entry, digital assets, sustainable finance, trade and project finance, outsourcing, enterprise risk and legal operations, data privacy, ethical AI and environmental, social and governance (ESG) integration.

Layla worked at a leading international bank's regional headquarters in the Dubai International Financial Centre (DIFC) from 2014-2025 where she was selected by management for Top Arab Talent and Women Leadership SOAR programs.
